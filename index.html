Bad Life Simulator

What is Bad Life Simulator?

Bad Life Simulator is an open-source, web-based, text-based life simulator inspired by BitLife and The Sims.


Why Bad Life Simulator?

Bad Life Simulator is community focused and community driven. This means no paywalls or microtransactions, ever, and the community gets the features the community wants.


Where can I play it?

The game hasn't been released yet, but it's not too far away all things considered. If you want to stay in the loop and be one of the first to get access, join the discord server.


I want to contribute. How can I do that?

I'm currently recruiting volunteer writers, designers, and programmers who want to help out with development. Remember, you won't get paid, as this is an open-source project. If you want to help out, or just throw around a couple of ideas, join the discord server.


Thank you so much for your interest in Bad Life Simulator. I hope you enjoy it when it comes out :)
